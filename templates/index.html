Documentation

PORTRAY (Product Ranker and Trend Analyser) mitigates a major challenge faced by Fashion retailers across the world, it acts as a one stop destination that identifies the latest fashion trends and consumer sentiments on e-commerce and fashion magazine platforms for any consumer defined product category. Why use it?

  • The algorithm takes over the tedious and time consuming task of individually going through a plethora of websites and analyses them based on various parameters that are of prime importance to designers
  • As the whole process of extracting data becomes automated, there is a considerable increase in accuracy. The algorithm also has a better reach in terms of the range and the quantity of products analysed
  • Adaptive Scraping. We can add a new product from a already implemented website in 15 minutes. We can add a complete website in 30.

How to use PORTRAY to best suit your needs.

We offer three features as described below.

  • 01 E-Commerce Based Product Ranking.

    You choose a website from our selection. You provide us a product vertical. Our algorithm analyses -> Reviews, Weights every review, Views, Ratio of Ratings to Views etc to provide you the top 5 and bottom 5 products right now. Not only that, we give you a comprehensive analysis of features that you must use to design the next internet sensation.

  • 02 Fashion Blogs Based Trend Analysis

    We scour over 100 blogs and articles related to your product vertical. We use that to determine which ecommerce based products are more likely to be "TRENDY" right now. We use multiple factors like date, endorsements etc to also analyse the current general trends.

  • 03 Any Additions?

    You can also request the addition of any webisite or product vertical. We guarentee addition in 30 mins - 1 hour. Oue adaptive scraping methods can be applied to both dynamic and static websites to prevent data loss.
